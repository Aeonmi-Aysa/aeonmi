## 📊 EXAMPLES INCLUDED

### 1. Hello Quantum (`hello_quantum.ai`)
**Concept:** Bell state entanglement  
**Outcome:** Only |00⟩ and |11⟩ appear (proof of entanglement)  
**Learning:** Superposition, CNOT gate, measurement

### 2. Grover's Search (`grover_search.ai`)
**Concept:** Quantum database search  
**Outcome:** Find target in √N steps (quadratic speedup)  
**Learning:** Oracle, diffusion operator, quantum advantage

### 3. Quantum Fourier Transform (`qft_pattern.ai`)
**Concept:** Frequency analysis of quantum states  
**Outcome:** Detect periodic patterns  
**Learning:** Basis transformation, Shor's algorithm foundation

### 4. 3-Qubit GHZ (`entanglement_demo.ai`)
**Concept:** Maximum 3-way entanglement  
**Outcome:** Only |000⟩ and |111⟩ appear  
**Learning:** Multi-qubit entanglement, Bell inequality violation
