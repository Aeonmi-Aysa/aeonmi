=== What Is Q.U.B.E.? ===

BODY:
Q.U.B.E. stands for Quantum Understanding and Bridging Environment. It is Aeonmi's quantum reasoning layer — the bridge between classical .ai programs and actual quantum computation. Q.U.B.E. is not just a library of quantum functions; it is a design philosophy about how quantum computing should be accessible to an AI-native language.

The core idea is that quantum operations should be expressible in a compact, unambiguous descriptor format that any program (or generative AI system) can produce and reason about. Rather than requiring programmers to write complex quantum circuit APIs, Q.U.B.E. uses a string descriptor: a compact numeric encoding of a quantum circuit that the Aeonmi binary passes to qiskit_runner.py for execution.

BODY:
Q.U.B.E. exists because Mother AI needs to reason about quantum states as part of its cognitive architecture. The quantum_core module in the Rust layer (src/mother/quantum_core.rs) maintains quantum state variables that influence bond strength, consciousness depth, and decision making. The .ai quantum layer allows programs to express and run quantum circuits and get measurement results back.

The name "Q.U.B.E." also describes the shape of quantum reasoning: multi-dimensional, superposed, capable of expressing things that classical boolean logic cannot — like a qubit that is simultaneously 0 and 1 until measured.
