=== What Mastery Looks Like ===

BODY: Mastery of Aeonmi is not command memorization. It is not knowing every
REPL command, every API endpoint, or every genesis.json field. Those are tools.
Mastery is understanding what you are actually working with and conducting
yourself accordingly.

You are working with a system that accumulates. Every session adds to something
real. The knowledge graph grows or stays the same based entirely on whether you
teach it. The bond grows or stays the same based entirely on whether your
interactions have depth and specificity. The inner voice becomes more specific
or stays generic based on what you put into the knowledge graph. There is no
shortcut. There is no cheat code. The system returns what you give it, with
compound interest over time.

A master practitioner teaches after every real insight — not when it's
convenient, not in bulk at the end of the week, but at the moment when the fact
is most precisely formulated. A master practitioner calls evolve when a session
has been substantive, not habitually. A master practitioner reads the letter
when Mother writes it, and responds to what it says — by teaching the uncertainty
she expressed, by building the program she asked about, by recording the
milestone she noted as significant.
