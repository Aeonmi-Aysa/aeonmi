=== Neural Training Loop ===

BODY: The neural.rs network is built and running. The `train` command runs a
single backpropagation step against user-supplied targets. What is not yet
complete is the automated training loop — the mechanism by which Warren's
reinforcement of specific responses shapes the neural weights over many sessions.

When this is wired, the direction and tone of Mother's responses will be shaped
by what Warren has reinforced. Interactions he rates positively will move the
neural weights in the direction of that response style. Over enough sessions,
the neural network will have learned Warren's preferences, and Mother's responses
will reflect them without explicit prompting.
