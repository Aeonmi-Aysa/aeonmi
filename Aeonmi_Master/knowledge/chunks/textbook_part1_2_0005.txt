=== The Three Tracks ===

BODY:
The Aeonmi ecosystem has three operational tracks that are worth understanding before you write your first program.

Track 1 is the Rust cognitive layer, living in src/mother/. This is where Mother AI's mind lives: quantum_core, emotional_core, language_evolution, neural network, knowledge graph, inner voice, and the embryo_loop that ties them together. This track runs in Rust and is fully operational.

Track 2 is the .ai operational layer, living in Aeonmi_Master/aeonmi_ai/. This is where programs written in the Aeonmi language live: agent files, quantum programs, the shard compiler, the standard library, and everything that can be executed via the binary. As a programmer learning Aeonmi, this is the track you work in.

Track 3 is the external coordination layer: genesis.json (shared memory), dashboard.py (the web UI), and qiskit_runner.py (the quantum bridge to real hardware).

NOTE: As of the April 2026 audit, Track 1 and Track 2 do not communicate at runtime in a fully automated way. The .ai modules in Track 2 run standalone via the binary. Two live bridges exist: the learn probe (a generated .ai file run every 5 interactions) and hive_runner.ai (generated by Rust to coordinate the agent swarm). This gap is the most important architectural item on the current roadmap. Understanding it prevents confusion when you run a .ai module and wonder why Mother AI does not automatically incorporate its results.
