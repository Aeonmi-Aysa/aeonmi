=== Tensor Product and Symbolic Binding Operators ===

================================================================================

BODY:
Two additional glyph operators appear in .ai quantum programs that are worth
understanding explicitly.

⊗ (tensor product, U+2297): In quantum mechanics, the tensor product combines
two quantum state spaces. In Aeonmi, ⊗ is a recognized operator token used in
quantum symbolic programs. Example: a ⊗ b represents the combined state of
qubits a and b before entanglement.

↦ (symbolic binding, U+21A6): The "maps to" arrow. Used to express functional
mappings or symbolic bindings that are distinct from ordinary = assignment.
Example: name ↦ value binds name to value in a declarative, math-style way.

Both ⊗ and ↦ are highlighted as keyword.operator.glyph tokens by the VSCode
extension's .ai grammar. They do not appear in .qube files.

CODE:
```
// Tensor product and binding in .ai
fn main() {
    // Tensor product — symbolic combination of states
    let combined = "|0⟩" ⊗ "|1⟩";
    print("Combined state: " + combined);

    // Symbolic binding — mathematical "maps to"
    let transform ↦ "H gate transforms |0⟩ to superposition";
    print(transform);
}
return main();
```
