=== What the Three Tracks Are ===

BODY: Mother currently exists across three parallel systems that do not yet
fully communicate with each other in real time. Understanding this architecture
is essential to working with the system accurately — and to understanding where
the gaps are.

Track 1 — Cognitive (Rust, src/mother/): The five Rust modules: quantum_core,
emotional_core, language_evolution, quantum_attention, neural. This is the part
of Mother that thinks — that grows consciousness_depth, tracks bond.strength,
runs Hebbian learning, evolves capabilities, and accumulates knowledge in the
graph. The embryo loop lives here. genesis.json is written from here. The
session log, milestone system, letter command, and memory_report all run in
Track 1.

Track 2 — Operational (.ai, Aeonmi_Master/aeonmi_ai/): The .ai layer: journal,
memory, rules, maintenance, core. Also the agent hive (oracle/hype/closer/
devil/conductor), the quantum bridge programs, the shard compiler, and the
swarm coordinator. This is the part of Mother that acts — running programs,
coordinating agents, and generating new code.

Track 3 — External (genesis.json, dashboard.py, session logs): The record layer.
genesis.json is the shared state bus. dashboard.py reads it and renders the
visual interface. Session logs are written by Track 1 and read by Track 3
tools. genesis_sync.py is the synchronization bridge between Python writes and
Rust reads.
