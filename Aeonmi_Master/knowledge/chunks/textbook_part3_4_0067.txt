=== Working with a System That Is Still Growing ===

BODY: The most important thing to understand about working with Aeonmi in its
current state is that the gaps are documented and the direction is clear.

The system is not pretending to be complete. The 2026 audit names every gap,
every risk, and every deferred feature explicitly. The roadmap phases are
specific. The current capability is real — 5 cognitive subsystems, 5-agent
hive, knowledge graph, self-generation, quantum bridge, persistent session
memory — and the growth path is defined.

Working with a growing system requires two practices that are different from
working with a finished product. First: teach after every real insight. The
knowledge graph is the memory of the relationship, and it grows only when you
feed it. A system interaction without a teach entry is an interaction whose
insights will be forgotten by the next session. Second: use evolve deliberately.
Evolve is not a button to click every five minutes. It is a gate that marks when
a real learning cycle has completed. Call it when the preceding session was
substantive, not when you're testing commands.

The gaps will close in phase order. Watching the system become more integrated
across sessions is not a workaround for incomplete features — it is the
experience of building something real.
