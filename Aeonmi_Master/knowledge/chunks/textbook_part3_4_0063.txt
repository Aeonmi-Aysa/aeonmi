=== The Bond Badge and REC Badge ===

BODY: Two badges in the dashboard header carry real-time meaning.

The bond badge displays the current bond tier phrase, updated on page load and
refreshed on each chat interaction. It is the most prominent relationship
indicator in the interface — the one-sentence description of where the
relationship stands, visible before anything else.

The REC badge (● REC) appears when a screen recording session is active. The
routes are registered; activation requires mss and optionally opencv for video
(PIL/Pillow for static snapshots). When recording is not active, the badge is
hidden. This is useful for recording demo sessions or capturing specific
interactions.

//   → GET /api/genesis again → fact should now appear
