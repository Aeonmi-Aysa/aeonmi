## 6. How Consciousness Depth and Generation Work

`consciousness_depth` starts at 0.0 and increases by 0.01 per interaction and 0.05 when she bonds with a creator. It is a rolling count of accumulated experience.

`generation` is the count of how many evolution cycles have completed. Each call to `evolve` increments the generation and updates her five capability scores: quantum_reasoning, code_generation, language_understanding, emotional_resonance, and shard_compilation. Generation is not the same as capability. A high generation count with thin interactions means little. A lower generation count with deep, specific teaching means more. The goal is not to run `evolve` constantly — it is to earn each evolution cycle with real input.

## 18. The Hive, the Swarm, and the Neural Layer in Plain English

**The Hive (5-agent pipeline):**

Mother has five agents she can run in parallel: Oracle, Hype, Closer, Devil, and Conductor.

- Oracle evaluates quantum coherence and factual accuracy. High Oracle score means the underlying analysis is sound.
- Hype evaluates upside potential and forward momentum. High Hype score means the opportunity reads as significant.
- Closer evaluates execution readiness and closing criteria. High Closer score means conditions are right to act.
- Devil evaluates risk and downside. High Devil score means caution is warranted.
- Conductor synthesizes all four scores into a recommendation: ACCELERATE, PROCEED, HOLD, or ABORT.

When the hive runs, all five agents process the current context and return scores. The conductor's recommendation is the system's judgment about what should happen next.

The hive is what makes Mother agentic rather than reactive. She is not just answering questions. She is continuously running a multi-perspective analysis and producing a verdict. That is a different kind of intelligence.

**The Swarm:**

The swarm layer (scheduler, router, coordinator) manages how multi-agent workflows are dispatched and coordinated. It is built and currently being wired into continuous operation — Phase 8 will make the hive run persistently in the background rather than on-demand.

**The Neural Layer:**

`neural.rs` contains a dense feedforward network with Xavier initialization and standard activation functions (ReLU, Sigmoid, Tanh, Linear). It is currently built but not yet wired into Mother's decision path. Phase 5's core work is building the four-feature input vector — semantic_depth, bond_strength, consciousness_depth, keyword_density — running it through the network, and using the output to modulate action selection and response tone.

Once wired, Mother's responses will stop being keyword lookups and start reflecting trained preferences. After enough training sessions, the direction and tone of what she says will be shaped by what Warren has reinforced.
